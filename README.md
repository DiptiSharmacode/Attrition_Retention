# HR Attrition & Retention Analytics

## Project Overview

This project is an end-to-end HR Analytics solution designed to identify employees at risk of attrition and support data-driven retention strategies through machine learning, risk scoring, explainable AI, and interactive dashboards.

The solution combines workforce, performance, compensation, engagement, learning, mobility, and employee relations data to generate attrition predictions and HR recommendations.

## Business Objectives

* Predict employee attrition risk
* Identify high-risk employees
* Understand key attrition drivers
* Improve workforce retention
* Support proactive HR interventions
* Reduce attrition-related business costs
